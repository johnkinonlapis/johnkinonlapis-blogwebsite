Photograph

One of the finest methods to preserve memories, tell tales, and spread joy is through photos. There are a variety of motivations to take up photography, including the need to document your child's development in the best way possible, the want to find original Instagram photo ideas, and the desire to arrange a trip to a scenic destination with plenty of photo chances.

What is Photography?

Photography is the science, practice, and art of making long-lasting images by capturing light, either chemically or electronically using a light-sensitive substance like photographic film or an image sensor.


The two main goals of photography are to convey ideas and preserve fleeting moments in time. Since the development of photography, people have been able to make art utilizing light. We may now describe the present, recall the past, and envision the future thanks to photography.
